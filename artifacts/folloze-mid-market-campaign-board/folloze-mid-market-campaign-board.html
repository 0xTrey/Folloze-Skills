The mid-market gap

Your team knows the accounts to pursue. Getting a tailored destination live is the hard part.

Mid-market teams rarely have extra web, design, ops, and agency capacity sitting idle. The campaign idea is clear, but the launch path usually has too many handoffs.

Personalization should not require a new production line every time a segment matters.

Folloze gives marketers a governed way to turn the message, content, and audience logic they already have into experiences that feel specific enough to convert and structured enough for sales to act on.

Web backlog slows launches. Campaign teams wait for destinations instead of shipping when the audience is warm.
Generic nurture loses context. The same path treats every visitor like the same buyer with the same problem.
Sales follow-up lacks signal. Clicks alone do not tell the team what the account cared about or what should happen next.
