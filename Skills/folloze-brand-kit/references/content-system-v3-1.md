## Executive Summary

This update replaces older external language built around `AI orchestration`, `activation layer`, and named Folloze agents with buyer-clear language:

- Folloze helps teams target and convert key accounts.
- Your AI creates content. Folloze deploys, hosts, governs, personalizes, and captures signal.
- External display copy uses `Build. Activate. Signal.`
- External prose uses the pattern `[Your AI does X]. [Folloze does Y].`
- Agent names and `activation layer` are internal-only unless the audience already knows Folloze.

## Category Point Of View

Targeting and converting key accounts is the work. Folloze is how marketing teams do it with the personalization and speed AI now makes possible.

The job has not changed: find the right accounts, deliver the right message, win the deal. AI changes the how. Folloze turns AI-generated or team-generated content into live, governed, measurable account experiences.

Bridge:

> Folloze is how your content reaches your accounts. And how your accounts reach back.

## Core Value Proposition

Folloze helps teams target and convert key accounts.

Bring your AI to create. Folloze handles deploy, host, governance, personalization, and signal capture so every campaign learns from the last.

## Core Beliefs

1. More targeted messaging equals higher conversion.
2. AI accelerates personalization for teams that use it well.
3. Folloze meets teams where they are.

## Four Mechanics

1. Bring your own AI. Folloze is the execution platform for Claude, GPT, internal models, custom agents, and other content workflows.
2. Create personalized experiences in minutes. Brief in, live personalized microsite out, with brand governance applied.
3. Capture richer first-party signals. Folloze captures account, feature, use-case, persona, buying-group, and LLM-driven engagement signals.
4. Close the loop. Engagement data flows back into the stack so the next campaign targets proven-warm segments.

## Three Capabilities, Two Registers

Use plain language externally. Use internal names only in sales, product, or team contexts where the audience already knows Folloze.

| External capability | External description | Internal name |
|---|---|---|
| Build | Folloze builds personalized microsites, campaign emails, on-behalf sales emails, LinkedIn ads, and additional content types from a brief. | Campaign agent |
| Activate | Folloze deploys, schedules, segments, and routes content across the stack with brand governance applied. | Activation agent |
| Signal | Folloze captures first-party engagement signals and surfaces them to sales, SDRs, rev ops, and external workflows. | Insight agent |

Display copy:

> Build. Activate. Signal.

Prose pattern:

> Your AI does X. Folloze does Y.

| Mode | Description |
