# Product Capabilities Public-Safe Reference

Use this reference when Folloze product capability context must be safe for a public repository, public prompt package, or no-Drive environment.

This file is intentionally stricter than the internal and customer-ready references. It contains generic capability definitions only. It excludes pricing, package rows, current credit quantities, burn rules, roadmap commitments, private Drive sources, order forms, customer-specific examples, and internal data-provider strategy.

## Public Positioning

Folloze helps B2B teams create personalized buyer experiences, activate those experiences across revenue motions, and capture first-party engagement signals.

Preferred plain-language frame:

- Build personalized experiences.
- Activate them across campaign and sales channels.
- Signal what buyers care about through engagement data.

## Public Capability Summary

### Campaign Builder

A no-code workspace for creating personalized buyer experiences such as microsites, campaign destinations, event hubs, content centers, resource pages, and sales rooms.

### AI-Assisted Generation

AI-assisted creation tools can help teams draft, rewrite, personalize, translate, localize, or generate content faster. Published output should still be reviewed and governed by the customer team.

### Personalization

Personalization adapts content, messaging, recommendations, CTAs, and experience paths based on available audience, account, source, behavior, or identity context.

### Account And Contact Intelligence

Account and contact intelligence uses available data and research to improve targeting, segmentation, personalization, routing, and follow-up.

### Enrichment

Enrichment improves account and contact context with additional attributes that can help teams create more relevant experiences and better follow-up.

### Content Engine

The content engine helps teams organize approved content, reuse assets across experiences, personalize content paths, and measure engagement with content.

### Integrations

Integrations connect Folloze experiences with marketing automation, CRM, ABM, data, sales, and reporting systems where configured.

### Enterprise Scale

Enterprise-scale capabilities can include localization, governed asset management, data access for analytics, and identity or access controls.

### Website Engagement

Website engagement can bring Folloze-powered personalized experiences into a company's existing web journey.

### Events

Folloze can support digital destinations for event promotion, event engagement, and post-event follow-up.

### User Roles

Creator and collaborator-style roles help teams separate experience creation, campaign management, sales participation, and engagement visibility.

### AI/Data Usage

Some AI and data workflows may use metered credits or usage allowances. Exact quantities and usage rules should come from approved commercial sources.

## Public Boundaries

- Do not include package entitlements, user counts, included credit quantities, or pricing-guide detail.
- Do not include internal Google Doc links as source evidence in public-facing output unless explicitly approved.
- Do not imply roadmap delivery, connector availability, or implementation scope without current confirmation.
- Do not expose private local paths, customer notes, Drive provenance, deal strategy, or source-system snippets.
- Use `where configured`, `where enabled`, or `available by package and configuration` when scope may vary.
