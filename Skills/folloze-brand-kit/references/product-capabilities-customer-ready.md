# Product Capabilities Customer-Ready Reference

Use this reference when drafting customer-facing Folloze content, sales docs, one-pagers, recap notes, board copy, or deal-room copy that needs product capability language.

This is customer-ready language, not a pricing guide. It intentionally avoids internal source notes, local paths, private Drive provenance, customer-specific terms, and exact package entitlements.

## Positioning

Folloze helps revenue teams create personalized buyer experiences, activate those experiences across campaign and sales motions, and capture first-party engagement signals that help teams decide what to do next.

The simplest external frame is:

- Build personalized account experiences.
- Activate them across campaign, sales, web, and event motions.
- Capture engagement signals that guide follow-up.

## Campaign Builder

The Campaign Builder is the no-code workspace for creating and publishing Folloze experiences. Teams use it to build personalized microsites, campaign destinations, event hubs, resource centers, sales rooms, and account-specific content experiences without waiting on web or development cycles.

Typical functions:

- Create branded buyer experiences from templates and reusable sections.
- Add content, videos, CTAs, forms, contact cards, and gated or ungated assets.
- Configure access, routing, personalization, and tracking.
- Publish destinations for email, ads, sales outreach, events, partner campaigns, and website engagement.
- Capture engagement at the account, visitor, content, CTA, and source level.

## Generator Agents

Generator Agents help teams move from a campaign brief or prompt to a usable first draft faster. They can assist with copy generation, rewriting, personalization, translation, localization, and video creation where those capabilities are enabled.

Use this framing with customers: AI accelerates creation, while teams still review, refine, approve, and publish the final experience.

## Personalization Engine

The Personalization Engine changes what a buyer sees based on available context such as account, industry, persona, role, source, stage, region, behavior, or known identity.

It can support different content paths, messaging, CTAs, banners, recommendations, and follow-up triggers for different audiences. The goal is to make the same campaign more relevant to each buying group instead of forcing every visitor through one generic path.

## Account And Contact Intelligence

Account and Contact Intelligence helps teams improve targeting, personalization, and follow-up by adding useful context to accounts and people.

It can include enrichment, research, audience planning, and signal interpretation depending on package, connected systems, and configuration.

## Enrichment

Enrichment improves account and contact records with additional context such as firmographics, industry, company size, domain, location, role, persona, CRM fields, MAP fields, ABM segment membership, intent data, and prior engagement signals.

Inside Folloze, enrichment is valuable because better data can improve segmentation, personalization, routing, reporting, and sales follow-up.

## Account And Contact Research

Research turns raw account and contact information into practical campaign context. It helps teams understand why an account matters, which message may resonate, which buying group is relevant, and which content path should be used.

Common outputs include account context, initiative signals, suggested messaging angles, persona assumptions, content recommendations, and sales follow-up notes.

## Audience Builder

Audience Builder refers to the capability area for assembling, filtering, and activating target audiences from available account, contact, CRM, MAP, ABM, enrichment, intent, and engagement data.

Confirm current availability, roadmap status, and package fit before describing it as a committed customer entitlement.

## Content Engine

The Content Engine is the governed content layer. It helps teams organize approved content, reuse assets across experiences, personalize content paths, recommend relevant assets, and measure what buyers consume.

Typical functions:

- Store and organize approved content.
- Reuse content across many boards and campaigns.
- Update shared content centrally.
- Recommend content based on account, stage, topic, profile, or behavior.
- Track content views, engagement duration, CTA clicks, and journey progress.

## Integrations

Folloze integrations connect buyer experiences with the systems revenue teams already use. These can include marketing automation, CRM, CDP, ABM, sales engagement, data, DAM, and reporting systems depending on package and configuration.

The integration story is a loop:

- Use existing customer data to target and personalize experiences.
- Capture buyer engagement inside Folloze.
- Send engagement signals back to connected systems for sales follow-up, nurture, reporting, and optimization.

## CRM Integrations

CRM integrations make Folloze engagement visible and actionable where sales teams work. They can help associate activity with accounts, contacts, leads, campaigns, opportunities, tasks, alerts, dashboards, and seller follow-up workflows.

Confirm object mapping, sync direction, sync frequency, custom fields, and supported CRM scope during implementation.

## Global Scalability

Global Scalability is the enterprise rollout layer for teams that need governance across regions, brands, business units, and security requirements.

It can include:

- Localization for regional messaging and language variants.
- Governed asset management or DAM-connected workflows.
- Data warehouse access for deeper analytics.
- SSO for identity, access control, and enterprise security.

Availability depends on package and configuration.

## Website Engagement

Website Engagement lets companies use Folloze experiences or embedded board sections as part of their existing website journey. It can turn the corporate website into a personalized launch point for account-based, inbound, resource center, event, and sales motions.

Public product language may also refer to the feature as Website Engager.

## Events

Events are Folloze-powered experiences for webinars, field events, virtual events, hybrid events, executive programs, roadshows, customer events, and post-event follow-up.

Teams can use Folloze to create a governed digital destination before, during, and after the event, then use engagement data to guide nurture, retargeting, sales follow-up, and account scoring.

## Creators And Collaborators

Folloze user licenses control what users can do.

Creator users typically build boards, assemble content, run campaigns, and access the platform capabilities included in the company plan.

Collaborator users typically support sales-oriented workflows, sharing, engagement visibility, and follow-up. Specific permissions vary by license type and role.

Confirm included license quantities and types in the current order form or package documentation.

## Folloze AI/Data Credits

Folloze AI/Data credits are a usage allowance for metered AI and data capabilities. They can support generation, video, enrichment, research, audience intelligence, or related workflows where enabled.

Do not quote credit quantities, burn rates, rollover rules, overage policies, or add-on pricing unless you are using a current approved pricing or order-form source.

## Customer-Facing Boundaries

- Say `available by package and configuration` when a capability may vary.
- Do not promise roadmap features without Product-approved wording.
- Do not include internal source notes or private Drive provenance.
- Do not include exact package rows unless the customer is already in a pricing/order-form discussion.
- Do not describe AI as unchecked automation; keep the human review and governance layer clear.
