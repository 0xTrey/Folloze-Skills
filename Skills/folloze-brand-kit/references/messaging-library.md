# Messaging Library

Use this file for approved Folloze value props, proof pillars, reusable copy blocks, and account-message mapping.

## Core Message

Folloze helps teams target and convert key accounts.

Longer version:

> Bring your AI to create. Folloze deploys the content, hosts the experience, captures engagement signals, and helps every campaign learn from the last.

Bridge line:

> Folloze is how your content reaches your accounts. And how your accounts reach back.

## Capability Message Blocks

### Build

Use when the account cares about speed, lean teams, campaign bottlenecks, web backlog, agency handoffs, or doing more with current resources.

Core ideas:

- AI can create the content, but Folloze turns it into a live governed experience.
- Move from brief, prompt, or assets into microsites, campaign emails, on-behalf sales emails, LinkedIn ads, and additional destinations.
- Reuse governed structures instead of rebuilding every page or path.
- Keep marketing in control without waiting on web-team or dev cycles.

Copy options:

- Build personalized account experiences from a brief, prompt, or campaign asset set.
- Turn AI-generated content into microsites, campaign emails, and sales-ready destinations without rebuilding each page.
- Give lean teams a governed way to launch more account-specific campaign moments.
- Move from strategy to live campaign execution with fewer handoffs.

### Activate

Use when the account cares about personalization, buyer relevance, segmentation, known and unknown visitors, source paths, campaign routing, or regional and partner rollout.

Core ideas:

- Personalized content needs an activation path across channels and audiences.
- Folloze deploys, schedules, segments, and routes content with brand governance applied.
- Buyer journeys should respond to account, source, persona, stage, and known identity.

Copy options:

- Activate the right content for the right account, segment, source, and buying stage.
- Deploy account experiences across campaign, sales, partner, and web motions with governance intact.
- Personalize the path without forcing every buyer through the same form-heavy journey.
- Keep global, partner, and field programs consistent while still making them specific.

### Signal

Use when the account cares about sales follow-up, pipeline proof, first-party intent, LLM traffic, reporting, attribution, or turning engagement into action.

Core ideas:

- Engagement should tell marketing and sales what the buyer actually did.
- Sellers need to know who engaged, what they consumed, where they came from, and what should happen next.
- First-party engagement should feed the next campaign, sales sequence, SDR action, or workflow.

Copy options:

- Capture first-party signals that show who engaged, what they consumed, and what should move to sales.
- Turn account engagement into a clearer bridge from campaign activity to pipeline-ready follow-up.
- See feature interest, use-case exploration, persona behavior, source path, and buying-group heat.
- Feed engagement data back into the stack so the next move is based on what the account actually did.

### Govern

Use when the account cares about enterprise readiness, security, procurement, brand control, regional rollout, localization, partner/client programs, or repeatability.

Core ideas:

- Enterprise teams need speed with control.
- Governance should be visible early enough to avoid slowing approval.
- Repeatable campaign systems need brand, access, analytics, and content discipline.

Copy options:

- Move quickly without losing enterprise control.
- Package brand, security, and governance details early so approval can move in parallel.
- Standardize campaign execution across regions, teams, partners, and client programs.
- Launch global programs with a governed foundation.

## Modes Of Operation

Use mode language when it helps the buyer understand how the work gets done:

| Mode | Buyer-facing description |
|---|---|
| LLM-centric | Work in Claude or another LLM, then use Folloze MCP to deploy content with brand rules applied. |
| Stack-driven | Trigger Folloze from workflow tools such as Slack, Outreach, n8n, Salesforce, or Clay. |
| On-platform | Build and deploy directly in Folloze with templates, segment rules, and board structures. |
| Automated engine | Use deal or engagement signals to trigger microsites, rooms, and nurture paths automatically. |

These are not maturity stages. Most customers use two or three modes simultaneously.

## Message-Fit Matrix

Before writing page copy for account-specific assets, create this matrix:

| Account signal | Source | Folloze value prop | Buyer-facing claim | Page placement |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Rules:

- Use account signals from call notes, Salesforce, Drive, Gmail/Calendar, or public research.
- Use Folloze value props from this brand kit or approved current materials.
- Put only externally safe language in buyer-facing claims.
- Use the matrix to choose what goes into the layout.

The matrix prevents generic Folloze copy from crowding out the account's real reason to care.
