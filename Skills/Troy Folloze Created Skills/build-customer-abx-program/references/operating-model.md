# ABX Operating Model

## Technology roles

- 6sense is the account-intelligence and orchestration system.
- Folloze is the governed experience and first-party engagement layer.
- Salesforce is the account, opportunity, activity, amount, stage, contact-role, and next-step system of record.
- Marketo manages nurture, program membership, consent, scoring, and email.
- LinkedIn supports matched audiences, persona discovery, paid engagement, and event amplification.
- Outreach or Salesloft executes BDR sequences and records touches, responses, meetings, and dispositions.

Do not position the corporate website as a required late-stage validation gate.

## Growth motions

Run acquisition and expansion together:

- acquisition: net-new logo, category/problem discovery, high-fit cohort and verified surge;
- expansion: installed-base white space, adjacent teams/use cases, champion-forwardable experiences, renewal-adjacent growth.

## Stage safeguards

- Require ICP fit, ownership, capacity, suppression checks, and verified evidence before outreach.
- Do not route intent alone.
- Use combined account and person evidence.
- Treat anonymous activity as a research signal, not consent.
- Separate value hypotheses from CRM pipeline.

## Verified surge response

Within 24 hours:

1. detect and record the reason;
2. validate fit, suppression, owner, geography, capacity, timing, and first-party corroboration;
3. review 6sense stage/topics and Folloze engagement depth;
4. research the account and relevant people;
5. select the governed Folloze experience and approved sequence;
6. send evidence-based human outreach without revealing tracking;
7. log the action and disposition in Salesforce;
8. upgrade, continue, pause, or downgrade based on response.

## Buyer discovery focus

Prioritize:

- 6sense;
- LinkedIn;
- Marketo;
- BDR research and outreach;
- events and community;
- Folloze engagement.

Maintain AEO/GEO and community discovery in early stages.

## Crawl-Walk-Run

- Crawl: foundation, routing proof, first 10–15 sales-accepted opportunities.
- Walk: repeatable cohort programs and 25 additional opportunities.
- Run: regional/global scale and 60–65 additional opportunities.

Preserve clear monthly pacing from zero to the annual target.

## Measurement

- Foundation: source completeness, ownership, capacity, suppression.
- Target: account/persona coverage and acquisition/expansion mix.
- Awareness: 6sense movement, LinkedIn/event engagement, Folloze visits.
- Consideration: depth, recency, return engagement, multi-person activity.
- Decision: 24-hour SLA, research quality, response and meeting conversion.
- Opportunity: count, amount, stage, next step, contact roles, velocity.
- Program: cohort economics, channel contribution, pipeline, wins and bookings.
